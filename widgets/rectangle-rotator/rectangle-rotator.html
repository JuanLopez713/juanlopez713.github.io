Rectangle Symmetries (D2)

What is this group?

The symmetries of a non-square rectangle form a group with four elements: identity e, a 180° rotation r, a vertical flip v (left↔right), and a horizontal flip h (top↔bottom). Each non-identity has order 2, and combining any two different non-identities gives the third one.
