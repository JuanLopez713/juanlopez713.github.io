Nonogram

How to Play

Fill cells to reveal a hidden picture using the numbers beside each row and column. Each number tells you the size of a contiguous group of filled cells in that order, with at least one empty cell between groups.

Left-click to fill a cell, right-click to place an X. Click-and-drag to paint quickly. Dragging over an already filled/X cell will clear those marks when you start on that type.
